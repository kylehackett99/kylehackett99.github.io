ABOUT US


The Hourwork Universal Study Assistant Software is a software for users to take notes on another level. Notes are converted into flashcards that connects to Mind maps. The breakdown of these components and use of spaced repetition allows users to better understand concepts and retain information .

Download

HOW TO USE


Click on the Download icon to download our test file.

1. On the home page, click on upload file and upload the sample file.
2. The flashcard will be on the leftside with a previous(left) and next(right) button.
There will also be three buttons to be selected based off your understanding: Easy, Medium, and Hard.
3. The Mind Map will be on the right side. Each node will be connected to a flashcard. Users are able to click on each node which would prompt the flashcard to the left.



BROWSER REQUIREMENT


Any browser that runs Javascript ES5 and above.
